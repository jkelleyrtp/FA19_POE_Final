POE 2019: Team Real Steel

World's coolest shadow boxing robot

With love, from Olin College's 2019 Principles of Engineering class

Real-time pose estimation

Real Steel uses the Microsoft Kinect to perform real-time pose estimation for an immersive shadow boxing experience. Never before has a robot felt so similar to humans.

8 degrees of freedom

The Real Steel shadow-boxing robots come with 8 degrees of freedom: 2 arms with shoulders and elbows and a base that allows the robot to bob and weave like no boxing robot before it.

Exceptionally awesome

With 8 motors, 4 custom slew bearings, 4 gears, custom encoder and quadrature boards, the Real Steel bots are mechabots of awesome in under just $250.
